STARFINDER GAME TERMS:

CR (Challenge Rating): Numeric rating indicating encounter difficulty
DC (Difficulty Class): Target number for dice rolls
EAC (Energy Armor Class): Defense against energy attacks
KAC (Kinetic Armor Class): Defense against physical attacks
BAB (Base Attack Bonus): Combat skill modifier
SP (Spell Points): Resource for casting spells
RP (Resolve Points): Hero points for special abilities
Stamina Points: Temporary HP that recovers with rest
UPBs: Universal Polymer Base crafting currency
d20: 20-sided die used for most rolls
